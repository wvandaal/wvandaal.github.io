About Me

Allow me to (re)introduce myself...

My name is Willem and I'm a web developer experienced in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, and responsive web design.

I recently graduated from Trinity College with degrees in Computer Science and Hispanic Studies. I first started designing for the web in the winter of 2013 and I haven't looked back. Of particular interest to me is the rapidly evolving landscape of web design, especially in the areas of Shadow DOM, Web Components, and Responsive Design.
